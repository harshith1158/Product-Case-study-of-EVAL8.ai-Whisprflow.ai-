# 📊 Product Teardowns & Strategic Reviews

Welcome! This repository contains my product teardown series, where I analyze real-world AI products from a **Product Management perspective**.

Rather than simply listing features, each review focuses on understanding:

* The problem the product is solving
* Target users and personas
* User journeys
* Product strategy
* Business value
* Trade-offs and product decisions
* Risks and assumptions
* Opportunities for future growth

The goal is to think like a Product Manager—not just a product user.

# My Product Thinking Framework

Whenever I analyze a product, I try to answer questions such as:

* What problem is the product actually solving?
* Who experiences this problem?
* Why does this solution work?
* Where does the product create value?
* What assumptions does it make?
* What are the biggest product risks?
* What differentiates it from competitors?
* How could the product evolve over the next few years?

I believe strong Product Management goes beyond feature analysis—it requires understanding users, business goals, and long-term strategy.

As an aspiring Product Manager with a technical background in AI and software development, I wanted to practice analyzing products the way product teams do internally.

Each teardown is an opportunity to improve my ability to:

* Break down complex products

* Think strategically about roadmaps
* Balance user value with business outcomes

This repository documents that learning journey.
